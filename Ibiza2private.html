questions

Do you offer private airport transfers in Ibiza?
Yes, 24/7. We pick you up directly at Ibiza Airport (IBZ) arrivals and take you to any hotel, villa, yacht or address on the island. Fixed price, flight tracking included.
What areas of Ibiza do you cover?
We cover the entire island: Ibiza Town, San Antonio, Santa Eulària, Playa d'en Bossa, Portinatx, Sant Josep, private villas and remote calas. No location is too far.
How do I book a private driver in Ibiza?
Simply contact us via WhatsApp or fill in the booking form. We respond within minutes, 24 hours a day. We confirm immediately.
Do you offer hourly chauffeur service?
Yes. You can hire our private driver by the hour for full island exploration, events, business meetings or simply to have a personal driver at your disposal throughout your stay.
Are prices fixed or do they use a meter?
All our prices are fixed and agreed before travel. No meters, no traffic surcharges, no hidden costs. What we quote is what you pay.
